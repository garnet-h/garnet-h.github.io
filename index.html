About Garnet.H

Garnet.H is an interior design app for home rooms, including bedrooms, children's rooms, living rooms, and even kitchens. You can specify details such as materials, colors, electrical appliances, lighting, and more. The design is executed after you review and approve one of the quotes provided by the designers.
